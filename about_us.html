About Us

Welcome to the TCU Online Publication, where we aim to provide up-to-date news and events for the students and community of Taguig City University. Our platform serves as a space for students to stay informed and engaged with what’s happening in their university.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a dynamic online space where the student community can access reliable information, share ideas, and celebrate the achievements of their peers. We strive to uphold transparency, integrity, and inclusivity in all our publications.

Our Goals

Our primary goals are to:

  • Provide timely and accurate news that reflects the interests of the student body.
  • Encourage student participation in content creation to foster a sense of ownership and community.
  • Highlight student achievements and promote academic and extracurricular activities.
  • Serve as a bridge between students and the university administration.

Community Engagement

We believe in the power of community engagement. We regularly host events, workshops, and discussions to encourage students to get involved and share their perspectives. Our publication is more than just a source of news; it’s a platform for dialogue and collaboration.
